Jianan Lu

I am a first-year Ph.D. student studying Computer Science in Princeton SNS Group. My research interets are systems, security and applied cryptography.

Before joining Princeton, I spent five wonderful years at UC Berkeley, where I received my B.A. and M.S. in Computer Science. During my M.S., I was advised by Raluca Ada Popa. I also worked closely with Rishabh Poddar and Stephanie Wang from RISELab, Amit Datta from Snap Inc. and Michael Carl Tschantz from International Computer Science Institute (ICSI).
